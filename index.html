Our Mission

When your people are well, your organization performs — and when your organization is healthy, your community feels it too.

Most organizations know something isn't working. People are burning out. Good employees are leaving. Leadership feels stuck. The culture doesn't reflect what you actually stand for.

Balhance exists to help you fix that — not with a generic program or a one-size-fits-all framework, but with a clear-eyed look at what's actually going on and a practical path to something better.

We work with growing companies and mission-driven organizations who understand that investing in their people isn't just the right thing to do. It's what makes everything else work.

Insight Over Assumption
We find out what is actually driving your people challenges before recommending anything. No guesswork.
The Whole Picture
Employee well-being, organizational health, and community impact are connected. We work at all three levels.
Built to Last
We don't hand you a report and leave. We work alongside your leadership until the change is real and sustainable.

What We Do

Three ways to work together.

Whether you need a clearer picture of what's happening inside your organization, a strategic partner for your leadership team, or a coaching relationship that helps you grow as a leader — Balhance meets you where you are. Every engagement is built around your specific situation, not a template.

01

Organizational Research

Get clear on what's really going on. You can't fix what you don't understand — so we go inside your organization to find the patterns that leadership often can't see from the top.

  • A clear diagnosis of root causes, not symptoms
  • An honest picture of how culture, structure, and leadership affect performance
  • Specific, prioritized recommendations your team can act on
  • Confidence that decisions are based on real insight
  • A foundation for any culture, strategy, or leadership change
02

Leadership Advisory

A strategic partner for your leadership team. Balhance works alongside senior leaders as a trusted advisor — helping you think more clearly, make better decisions, and build an organization that is genuinely sustainable.

  • Honest outside perspective on your biggest challenges
  • Culture and organizational health strategy grounded in what your people need
  • Support navigating growth, restructuring, and leadership transitions
  • Clear frameworks for better decisions about people and culture
  • Ongoing advisory access for organizations in sustained change
03

Leadership Coaching

One-on-one coaching for leaders who want to grow. This is a space to slow down, get honest, and build the kind of leadership that lasts — not about fixing something broken, but becoming more intentional and resilient.

  • A confidential space for your biggest leadership challenges
  • Clearer sense of who you are as a leader and how you want to show up
  • Better tools for managing stress, pressure, and senior leadership demands
  • Stronger relationships with your team and peers
  • Confidence to lead through change and uncertainty without burning out

How We Work

We listen before we recommend. Always.

Every organization is different. The challenges that look the same on the surface almost never have the same root cause. That's why we don't arrive with a pre-built answer. Every Balhance engagement starts with genuine curiosity about your specific situation — your people, your history, your goals, and the pressures your leadership is navigating.

01

Listen First

We start by understanding your organization, not pitching a solution. That means real conversations with you and your leadership team about what's happening, what you've tried, and what you actually need.

02

Find the Real Story

We go deeper than surface symptoms — gathering information from across your organization to understand what's actually driving the challenges you're facing.

03

Make Sense of It

We turn what we learn into a clear, honest picture that your leadership team can understand and act on. No jargon. No 80-page report that sits on a shelf.

04

Work on It Together

We don't hand you a plan and disappear. We work alongside your team to put recommendations into practice — adjusting as you go.

05

Make Sure It Sticks

We track what's changing and what isn't, so the work keeps producing results over time — not just in the months after an engagement.

Leadership Coaching

Balance begins at the top.

The culture of any organization reflects the people leading it. When leaders are stretched thin, reactive, and running on empty — their teams feel it. When leaders are clear, grounded, and intentional — that spreads too.

Balhance leadership coaching is built for leaders who want to show up at their best — for their teams, their organizations, and themselves. It's a serious commitment to growth, and it shows in the results.

01
Individual Leadership Coaching
One-on-one sessions focused on you — your strengths, your blind spots, your goals, and what gets in the way. Delivered virtually or in person.
02
Leadership Team Programs
Group sessions that help your senior team work better together — building trust, shared language, and aligned habits around what it means to lead well in your organization.
03
Ongoing Advisory Access
Retainer-based support for organizations that want consistent, long-term coaching and advisory as they grow and change.

Focus Areas

  • Performing at a high level without running yourself into the ground
  • Managing the emotional weight of leadership
  • Building trust and psychological safety with your team
  • Leading through uncertainty, change, and tough decisions
  • Being the kind of leader your people want to follow

About

This work is personal.

I started Balhance because I've spent my career at the intersection of leadership, organizational change, and human well-being — and I know what it costs when organizations get that balance wrong.

I work with mid-size organizations and nonprofits who are serious about that kind of change.
