Our Mission

Advancing well-being as a strategic imperative.

Balhance was founded on a simple but powerful conviction: organizations that invest in the whole person outperform those that do not. We translate that conviction into measurable outcomes — combining academic rigor with practical wisdom to help leadership teams build cultures where people genuinely flourish.

We are not a wellness vendor. We are a research-driven advisory practice that works closely with C-suite leaders and HR executives to diagnose the real drivers of imbalance, design evidence-based interventions, and sustain meaningful change over time.

Confidential

How We Work

A structured path from insight to impact.

Our methodology integrates decades of occupational health research with practical business advisory — delivering not just diagnoses, but durable organizational change.

01

Discovery & Scoping

We begin with a deep listening session — understanding your organization's context, goals, and the specific challenges your people are facing.

02

Research & Diagnosis

Using validated instruments and custom frameworks, we gather quantitative and qualitative data across all relevant organizational levels.

03

Analysis & Insight

Our team synthesizes findings into a clear picture of well-being drivers and barriers, with prioritized recommendations for leadership action.

04

Implementation & Coaching

We work alongside your leadership team to put recommendations into practice — including coaching support to sustain behavioral change.

05

Measurement & Refinement

We track progress against agreed metrics and iterate the approach based on what's working — ensuring your investment in balance compounds over time.

Leadership Coaching

Balance begins at the top.

Leaders set the tone for everything. When executives model balance — genuinely, not performatively — it gives permission for the entire organization to do the same. Our leadership coaching practice is built on this conviction.

We work with individual leaders and leadership teams to develop the self-awareness, skills, and habits that sustain high performance without sacrificing well-being. Each coaching engagement is tailored to the specific pressures, strengths, and aspirations of the individual.

01
Individual Executive Coaching

02
Senior Leadership Team Programs

03
Ongoing Advisory Support
